Mako Shark

The shortfin mako is on record as the fastest-swimming shark, capable of bursts of speed up to 18.8 metres per second (68 km/h; 42 mph)

  • Habitat: The Ocean, of course.
  • Feeding: Cephalopods and bony fish.
  • Range: ~36 miles a day.
  • Lifespan: M-29 yrs | F-32 yrs.

The mako shark is an extremely agile and fast animal that can travel long ranges in search of food or mates.
